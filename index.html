About Nestlé

Nestlé is the world's largest food and beverage company, committed to improving quality of life and contributing to a healthier future. Founded in 1866, Nestlé operates in over 180 countries, providing nutritious, safe, and high-quality products.

Our mission is simple: Good Food, Good Life. We aim to help people live healthier lives while caring for the environment and communities.
